Riri's Favorite Memories

We've shared countless memories and had numerous dates; there are so many that I can't even count or list them all because each one was filled with joy and greatness. Whenever I'm with you, it feels as if a weight has been lifted from my chest, and I'm eternally grateful for the wonderful moments we've created together. I'll carry those memories with me forever, and please know that, in order to preserve them, I'll continue to live—for you.

One of the most cherished memories etched in my heart is our initial encounter when I delicately clasped your hand. I remember vividly the tremor in your grasp, and I found it utterly endearing, even though I wasn't in the best condition that day. My concern was not to burden you with my worries, and yet, a persistent fear lingered—what if you realized that you didn't truly fancy me after that day? Another memory surfaces when I embraced you, and you didn't appear entirely at ease. I comprehended your hesitation, and it's astounding to witness the remarkable transformation since that moment. Presently, you exude affection and embrace a clinginess that warms my heart. That bus ride remains ensconced as the most enchanting experience; every second felt like I was soaring on cloud nine. Even now, it retains the ethereal quality of a beautiful dream, a dream that continues to captivate my senses.

Our First Bus Ride Together

From the moment we crossed paths, I never fathomed that we would experience the magic of sharing a kiss. That initial embrace of our lips marked the pinnacle of enchantment in my life. The tenderness of your lips, redolent with the fragrance of a gentle breeze, left an indelible impression. Your scent was so captivating, and the proximity of our beings was surreal—I could hear the rhythmic cadence of your breath and sense the reverberations of your heartbeat. The kiss we exchanged was not merely a meeting of lips; it was an exchange imbued with love, devoid of any hint of confusion, fear, or regret. It was a moment of absolute purity, a poignant reminder of the vitality that had been absent from my life for countless years. For the very first time, I experienced a profound sense of aliveness, a profound connection with you that breathed new meaning into my existence. It felt as though the fractured pieces of my being had at last discovered wholeness and completion in your embrace.
